Nautika Nautics

Tivat je primorski grad sa dobrom geostrateškom pozicijom i samim tim je nautika uvijek bila veoma razvijena u gradu. Kroz svoju istoriju igrao je veliku ulogu kao ratni arsenal, a danas je marina za luksuzne jahte, glisere i ostala prestižna plovila. Tivat is a coastal town with a good geostrategic position, and thus nautics has always been very developed in the city. Throughout its history, it played a significant role as a naval arsenal, and today it is a marina for luxury yachts, speedboats, and other prestigious vessels.

Danas najviše pažnje privlači Porto Montenegro kao luksuzna marina, to jest pristanište za strane turiste i mjesto u kojem, pored pristajanja, mogu i odmoriti, pa i živjeti. Today, Porto Montenegro attracts the most attention as a luxury marina, serving as a docking place for foreign tourists and a location where they can not only dock but also rest and even live.

Istorija nautike: History of Nautics:

Arsenal: Arsenal:

Arsenal Arsenal

1886. godine uočena je strateška povoljnost Tivta za izgradnju pomorskog arsenala. Komanda mornarice Austrougarske je 1888. godine otpočela otkup zemljišta i pripremne radove na njegovom nivelisanju. U interesu razvoja grada, Tivatska opština je 1889. godine donijela odluku da se Komandi austrougarske ratne mornarice besplatno ustupi zemljište za izgradnju arsenala, u kojem su mještani trebali imati prvenstvo zaposlenja. In 1886, the strategic advantage of Tivat for building a naval arsenal was recognized. The Austro-Hungarian Navy Command began purchasing land and preparatory leveling work in 1888. In the interest of city development, the Municipality of Tivat decided in 1889 to grant land free of charge to the Austro-Hungarian Navy Command for the construction of the arsenal, where locals were to have employment priority.

Stare podmornice: Old Submarines:

Stare podmornice Old Submarines

U sjeverozapadnom dijelu nautičko-turističkog centra Porto Montenegro u Tivtu, na platou ispred Zbirke pomorskog nasljeđa, nalazi se najveći i najatraktivniji muzejski eksponat ove kolekcije - velika, tzv. dizel-električna podmornica P-821 “Heroj” nekadašnje Jugoslovenske Ratne Mornarice (JRM). Uz "Heroja" je izložena i takozvana diverzantska podmornica "Una". In the northwest part of the nautical-tourist center Porto Montenegro in Tivat, on the plateau in front of the Maritime Heritage Collection, lies the largest and most attractive museum exhibit of this collection—the large diesel-electric submarine P-821 "Heroj" of the former Yugoslav Navy. Alongside "Heroj," the diver submarine "Una" is also exhibited.

Današnja nautika: Today's Nautics:

Muzej nautičkog nasljeđa: Maritime Heritage Museum:

Nautički muzej Maritime Museum

Smješten u restauriranoj pilani iz 19. vijeka, Muzej nautičkog nasljeđa Porto Montenegra slavi bogatu pomorsku istoriju Tivta, sa više od 300 eksponata, uključujući rijetku austrougarsku pomorsku opremu i mašine i par autentičnih jugoslovenskih podmornica. Posebne tematske izložbe održavaju se tokom cijele godine. Muzej se nalazi na sjevernom kraju sela Porto Montenegro. Located in a restored 19th-century sawmill, the Porto Montenegro Maritime Heritage Museum celebrates Tivat's rich maritime history, featuring over 300 exhibits, including rare Austro-Hungarian naval equipment and machinery, and a couple of authentic Yugoslav submarines. Special thematic exhibitions are held throughout the year. The museum is located at the northern end of the Porto Montenegro village.

Porto Montenegro: Porto Montenegro:

Porto Montenegro Porto Montenegro

Porto Montenegro je luksuzna marina za jahte i nautičko naselje koje se nalazi u Tivtu. U kompleksu se nalazi marina (450 vezova za jahte dužine od 12 do 250 metara), rezidencije, poslovni prostori, Hotel "Regent" sa 5 zvjezdica, Zbirka pomorskog nasleđa sa vrijednim muzejskim eksponatima, smještena u rekonstruisanoj zgradi nekada Austrougarske pilane, Jaht klub, Međunarodna škola "Knightsbridge School International Montenegro". Porto Montenegro is a luxury yacht marina and nautical village located in Tivat. The complex includes a marina (450 berths for yachts ranging from 12 to 250 meters), residences, commercial spaces, the 5-star "Regent" Hotel, a Maritime Heritage Collection with valuable museum exhibits housed in a reconstructed former Austro-Hungarian sawmill, a yacht club, and the "Knightsbridge School International Montenegro".

Luštica Bay: Luštica Bay:

Luštica Bay Luštica Bay

Luštica Bay je luksuzna marina za jahte i selo-marina koje se nalazi na poluostrvu Luštica u opštini Tivat. Sastoji se od marine, rezidencija, poslovnih prostora, prodavnica, hotela, privatnih bazena koji su namijenjeni za posjetioce mjesta. Luštica Bay is a luxury yacht marina and marina village located on the Luštica peninsula in the municipality of Tivat. It consists of a marina, residences, commercial spaces, shops, hotels, and private pools intended for visitors.
