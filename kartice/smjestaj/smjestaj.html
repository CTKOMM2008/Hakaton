Smještaj Accommodation

Tivat se odlikuje po mnoštvu kvalitetnih hotela koji već godinama dobijaju najbolje ocjene stranih i domaćih turista. Fantastični spoj morkse obale i planinskih pejzaža čini Tivat neizbježnom lokacijom za boravak ljudi. Tivat is distinguished by the multitude of quality hotels that have been receiving the best ratings from foreign and domestic tourists for years. The fantastic combination of the coast and mountain scenery makes Tivat an inevitable location for people to stay.

Sve vrste gostiju mogu doći da iskuse lijep život koji Tivat nudi i uz širok izbor smještaja mogu se sklopiti sa dušom grada i zavoljeti ga. All types of guests can come to experience the beautiful life that Tivat has to offer and with a wide selection of accommodation, they can connect with the soul of the city and fall in love with it.

Hotel Palma: Hotel Palma:

Hotel Palma Hotel Palma

Hotel Palma, kategorisan sa četiri zvijezdice, nalazi se na samoj obali mora, u srcu Tivta, sa predivnim pogledom na Tivatski zaliv. Hotel Palma, categorized with four stars, is located right on the seashore, in the heart of Tivat, with a beautiful view of the Tivat Bay.

Hotel La Roche: Hotel La Roche:

Hotel La Roche Hotel La Roche

Hotel La Roche je smješten na samo nekoliko koraka od mora. U pitanju je hotel koji je sinonim za hedonizam i zadovoljstvo i posebnu pažnju poklanja svakom gostu. Sagradjen je u klasičnom stilu sa modernim i luksuznim sizajnom enterijera u kome svi uživaju. Hotel La Roche is located just a few steps from the sea. It is a hotel that is synonymous with hedonism and pleasure and pays special attention to every guest. It is built in a classic style with a modern and luxurious interior design that everyone enjoys.

Hotel the Chedi: Hotel the Chedi:

Hotel The Chedi Hotel The Chedi

Hotel The Chedi Luštica Bay se nalazi pored mora u zalivu Trašte, na 14 km od centra Tivta i nudi privatnu plažu i bazen na otvorenom. Moderno uređene sobe i suite jedinice imaju pogled na planinu ili more. The Chedi Luštica Bay Hotel is located by the sea in Trašte Bay, 14 km from the center of Tivat and offers a private beach and outdoor pool. Modernly decorated rooms and suites have a view of the mountain or the sea.
