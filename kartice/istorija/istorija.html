Istorija History

Tivat je prema mišljenjima istoričara najmlađi grad u Boki Kotorskoj, pa i na Crnogorskom primorju, no i dalje ima veoma bogatu istoriju. According to historians, Tivat is the youngest town in the Bay of Kotor and the Montenegrin coast, yet it still has a very rich history.

Prema javnosti poznatim podacima, prva epoha u razvoju Tivta koju karakteriše ilirski period, završava se 168-7. godine p.n.e. Iako o tom periodu danas svjedoči tek manji broj arheoloških nalaza, to vremensko razdoblje istorijski je bitno zbog činjenice da je tada propala ilirska država. According to publicly known data, the first epoch in Tivat's development characterized by the Illyrian period ends in 168-7 BC. Although only a small number of archaeological findings testify to that period today, this time frame is historically significant due to the fact that the Illyrian state collapsed then.

Spomenici: Monuments:

Spomenik narodnim herojima Milanu Spasiću i Sergeju Mašeri: Monument to National Heroes Milan Spasić and Sergej Mašera:

Razarač "Zagreb" Destroyer "Zagreb"

Potapanje razarača „Zagreb“ i pogibija te dvojice mornaričkih oficira. Spasić i Mašera su 17. aprila 1941, ne priznajući kapitulaciju Kraljevine Jugoslavije u Aprilskom ratu i odbivši naređenje da brod predaju neprijatelju, žrtvujući svoje živote, digli u vazduh razarač „Zagreb“ i sa njim potonuli u Tivatskom zalivu. The sinking of the destroyer "Zagreb" and the death of the two naval officers. On April 17, 1941, not recognizing the capitulation of the Kingdom of Yugoslavia in the April War and refusing the order to surrender the ship to the enemy, Spasić and Mašera sacrificed their lives by blowing up the destroyer "Zagreb" and sinking with it in the Bay of Tivat.

Spomenik palim borcima NOB-a: Monument to Fallen Fighters of the National Liberation War:

4. Jul 4th of July

4. jul - Dan borca, koji proslavljamo u znak sjećanja na 4. jul 1941. godine, kada je Politbiro Centralnog komiteta KPJ, na čelu sa Josipom Brozom Titom, donio odluku o otpočinjanju oružanog ustanka naroda Jugoslavije protiv fašističkog okupatora. Narodi Jugoslavije su se masovno odazvali tom pozivu i krenuli u borbu protiv okupatora, u borbu za slobodu, koja je došla četiri godine kasnije. July 4th - Fighter's Day, which we celebrate in memory of July 4, 1941, when the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Yugoslavia, led by Josip Broz Tito, decided to start an armed uprising of the people of Yugoslavia against the fascist occupier. The peoples of Yugoslavia massively responded to that call and went into battle against the occupier, a fight for freedom that came four years later.

Spomenik stogodišnjice Arsenala: Monument of the Arsenal Centennial:

Arsenal Arsenal

Spomenik je podignut u čast 100 godina Mornaričko-tehničkog remontnog zavoda "Arsenal". Arsenal je otvoren 1889, a spomenik je podignut 100 godina kasnije, 1989. The monument was erected in honor of the 100th anniversary of the Naval Technical Repair Institute "Arsenal". The Arsenal was opened in 1889, and the monument was erected 100 years later, in 1989.

Spomen ploča u čast iskrcavanja Mornaričke Pješadije 2. Dalmatinske Brigade: Memorial Plaque Honoring the Landing of the Naval Infantry 2nd Dalmatian Brigade:

Iskrcavanje brigade Brigade Landing

Spomen obilježje se nalazi na kući u mjestu Lepetane, podignuto u čast iskrcavanja Mornaričke Pješadije II Dalmatinske Brigade u Lepetanima 1944. godine. Lokacija je na proširenju neposredno uz pristanište za trajekte u Lepetanima. The memorial is located on a house in Lepetane, erected in honor of the landing of the Naval Infantry 2nd Dalmatian Brigade in Lepetane in 1944. The location is on an expansion area directly next to the ferry dock in Lepetane.

Spomen ploča obješenim rodoljubima: Memorial Plaque to Hanged Patriots:

Pogibija rodoljuba Patriots' Execution

Spomen obilježje se nalazi u mjestu Radovići, Krtole. Posvećeno je memoriji na stradale rodoljube koje su fašisti objesili dana 22. novembra 1943. godine na ovom mjestu. The memorial is located in Radovići, Krtole. It is dedicated to the memory of the fallen patriots whom the fascists hanged on November 22, 1943, at this place.
